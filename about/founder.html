About the Founder

Angela Dzifa Darkey

Founder & Principal Consultant

Angela Dzifa Darkey, founder of Thinking Cap Consult, Ghana, holds a BSc in Fashion Design (KNUST), an MSc in Marketing (KNUST), and an MBA from the University of East London.

In addition to her degrees, Angela has pursued extensive professional certifications across diverse fields, including Digital Marketing, Customer Service Excellence, Artificial Intelligence, Strategic Communication, Data Analytics & Visualization, Event Management, Promotion & Sponsorship, and Project & Innovation Management, and others from respected institutions such as the Institute of Digital Marketing & Communication (IDMC) and other international training bodies.

Her strong academic and professional foundation equips her to deliver practical, innovative, and globally aligned solutions. With Thinking Cap Consult, Angela bridges her dual expertise in fashion and business consulting, helping individuals, brands, and businesses, particularly fashion and creative entrepreneurs, achieve Purpose, Clarity, and Longevity.

Thinking Cap Consult Journey

Angela launched Thinking Cap Consult, Ghana, in November 2023, creating a unique consulting service that bridges the gap between fashion expertise and business acumen. The company offers two distinct but complementary paths: Fashion Consult and Business Growth Consult, specifically designed to serve the fashion and creative industries.
