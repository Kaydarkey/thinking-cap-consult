About the Founder

Angela Dzifa Darkey

Angela Dzifa Darkey, the Ghanaian born and based founder of Thinking Cap Consult, holds a BSc. Fashion Design degree from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST).

She believes that her strong background in Master of Business Administration from the University of East London, MSc. Marketing from KNUST and Professional Development Courses taken in Digital Marketing and Customer Service Excellence, Artificial Intelligence, Digital Marketing and Strategic Communication, Data Analytics and Visualization, Event Management, Promotion and Sponsorship, Project and Innovation Management from the Institute of Digital Marketing and Communication, strongly positions her for a career as a fashion and business growth consultant.

She took the decision to launch "Thinking Cap Consult, Ghana," a unique online consulting service offering two distinct paths: Fashion Consult and Business Growth Consult in November, 2023.
