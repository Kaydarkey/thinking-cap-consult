About Us

Thinking Cap Consult, is a unique online consulting service offering two distinct paths: Fashion Consult and Business Growth Consult.

As the name suggests, we see ourselves as the essential "thinking cap" for our clients. This business offers comprehensive solutions for clients: from helping them make informed fashion choices to building thriving, purposeful and long-lasting brands & businesses.

With Thinking Cap Consult, our services aim to leave clients not just knowledgeable and satisfied, but empowered to stand out in their chosen fields, as well as building fashion and creative brands & businesses that will stand the test of time.

We also provide exceptional value to our clients with minimal foundational knowledge, ensuring they see tangible results from our collaboration.

Thinking Cap Consult, Ghana, transcends mere services. We foster a vibrant online community where people can access resources and insights on fashion and business topics.

This is to help them gain valuable knowledge, stay ahead of the curve, and make informed decisions with us as their trusted source.

Our Vision

To become the leading online resource for fashion and creative entrepreneurs and individuals, empowering them to achieve their goals.

Our Mission

To empower fashion and creative entrepreneurs & individuals by unlocking their full potential through actionable insights, innovative strategies, and unwavering support in both fashion and business.

Our Logo

Our Logo

Our logo carries a powerful message. The circle split into two halves represents the union of two entities: our clients and our company. Together, they form a complete whole, highlighting the collaborative nature of our work.

The lower part of the bulb symbolizes the powerhouse of brilliant ideas, the mechanism that fuels creativity, innovation, and solutions. Its link to the brain emphasizes the intelligence and expertise that drive everything we do.

By combining these elements, our logo reflects our belief that true solutions are born when we and our clients work together.
