About The Founder

Angela Dzifa Darkey

Angela Dzifa Darkey, founder of Thinking Cap Consult, Ghana, holds a BSc in Fashion Design (KNUST), an MSc in Marketing (KNUST), and an MBA from the University of East London.

Her extensive professional certifications span Digital Marketing, Customer Service Excellence, Artificial Intelligence, Strategic Communication, Data Analytics & Visualization, Event Management, and Project & Innovation Management.

With Thinking Cap Consult, Angela bridges her dual expertise in fashion and business consulting, helping fashion and creative entrepreneurs achieve Purpose, Clarity, and Longevity.
